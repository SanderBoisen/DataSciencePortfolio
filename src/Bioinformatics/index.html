Project Description

I completed a course on the data science aspects of bioinformatics and these examples are from the final report. The course did not focus on bioinformatics algorithms or reads and sequences, but rather the data that follows along with it: Read lengths, depth of coverage, read window, comparison with simulations and links with statistics. I have chosen three of my favorite plots from the report. The full report is available below.

Plot details

  • Scatter plot of nucleotide diversity at sites coloured whether they display extreme values of two parameters.
  • Various parameters and distance to centromer
  • Test of whether or not cirtain regions of the genome adhere to a Poison null-distribution for seqeuencing coverage
