Plant-Pollinator interactions In the Rocky Mountains

Previous Next

Project Description

I was working with Paul Caradonna Ph. D. on implementing a phylogenetic aspect to his work with plant-pollinator interactions in the Rocky Mountains. Our collaboration was cut short by the beginning of my own Ph.D. project, but I used some of the available data in an exam project before then. Here I present some of the plots from that project, mostly made using ggplot and cowplot in r. A link to the full report follows below.

Item list

  • Comparison of Mean Pairwise Distances of phylogenetic tips on Species and Family level
  • Distribution of interactions of pollinator classes coloured by plant family
  • Distribution of pollinator observations coloured by pollinator family
