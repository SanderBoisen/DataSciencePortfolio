Project Description

One of my favorite disciplines in statistics and exploratory analysis is multivariat statistics. Phylogenetics are a special case of multivariat statistics and clustering in particular that deals with evolutionary data. The goal is often to produce a fully resolved phylogenetic tree that reflects evolutionary history of the studied group of organisms. Today sequence data is often used and each position in a gene represents a variable. Phylogenetic trees can therefore be thought of as a summary of the differences across millions or billions of variables.
