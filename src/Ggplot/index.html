ggplot A few plots produced during my research

Description

I have been using ggplot and the tidyverse regularly in my research and pastime projects. 'ggplot' provides both simplicity and power as plots are easily generated, but the potential for adjusting plotting variables is enormous. I have included three examples: A heatmap of habitat similarities based on 'geom_tile', a scatter plot showing the impact of growing wolf populations plotting as many variables as aestetic elements as possible, and a set of boxplots of model parameters of 100 different phylogenetic models

List of items

  • Heatmap of habitat similarities
  • Scatter plot of 4 variables
  • Boxplot of 5 parameters of 100 model runs
  • More examples to come
